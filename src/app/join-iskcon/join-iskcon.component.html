ADD VALUES

Add value to your life with the teachings of Srimad Bhagavad Gita. The Bhagavad Gita defines the way of life at the Philosophical and Spiritual levels through harmonized spiritual pursuits and the combination of Action, Knowledge, and Devotion (Karma, Gyaana, and Bhakti, respectively).

AWAKEN YOURSELF

The Bhagavad Gita is more than an ancient text. It is a song sung by Krishna which helps to find a solution to every problem of life. The mystical knowledge in this spiritual book helps in awakening yourself and see the absolute truth of life.

EXPERIENCE THE SERENITY

Bring peace in your life with the holy Bhagavad Gita. The verses mentioned in this sacred book give different levels of peace to the mind and make it stress-free. Add calmness with the valuable teachings of Bhagavad Gita.

Discover Yourself

Discover Your Self (DYS) is a short but life transforming course at the end of which you will discover a new "You" who has discovered a new meaning of life and happiness. This is a journey from difficulty to motivation from limitation to strength and confusion to enlightenment ! This program is presented by ISKCON Youth Forum.

Journey of Self Discovery

This course contains the essence of a study on the text and the principal religious ideas within the Bhagavad Gita. In this course, we have considered the Bhagavad Gita and tried to establish the main themes it pursues in its teachings, thereby seeking to identify the full significance of this famous scripture.

Gita Course
